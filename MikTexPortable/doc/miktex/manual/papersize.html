Setting the Preferred Paper Format

You can set the preferred paper format with the help of MiKTeX Options.

Click Start → Programs → MiKTeX 2.9 → Maintenance → Settings to open the MiKTeX Options window (see Figure 4.1, “MiKTeX Options: General Settings”). Select your preferred paper format from the drop-down list and click OK.
